## Usage Notes

1. **Fill in all sections** - Don't leave placeholders in final documents
2. **Be specific** - Use concrete examples and data
3. **Justify decisions** - Explain why choices were made
4. **Update iteratively** - Documents evolve as understanding deepens
5. **Maintain consistency** - Ensure alignment across all three documents
